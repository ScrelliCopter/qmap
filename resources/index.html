qmap - E1M1

Software rendered Quake 1 map viewer by Sean Barrett.
Updated & ported to emscripten by a dinosaur.
Source: github.com/ScrelliCopter/qmap

WASD - move around.
Q & E - roll view. (useless but neat)
Arrows - rotate the camera.
M - toggle mouselook.
P - cycle aspect ratio.
